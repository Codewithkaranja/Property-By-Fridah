Areas We Serve

Specialized knowledge of Kenya's fastest-growing property markets

Kitengela - The Family-Friendly Investment Hub

Kitengela has transformed from a small trading center to one of Nairobi's most sought-after satellite towns. Located just 30km south of Nairobi, it offers the perfect balance of urban convenience and peaceful living.

  • Prime Location: 30 minutes from Nairobi via Mombasa Road
  • Infrastructure: Excellent road network, shopping malls, schools, hospitals
  • Property Types: Gated communities, standalone homes, apartments, plots
  • Investment Potential: High appreciation rates, strong rental demand
  • Lifestyle: Family-friendly with parks, recreational facilities

Ngong – The Scenic Residential & Investment Hub

Ngong is known for its cool climate, scenic landscapes, and proximity to Nairobi, making it a prime location for residential living and long-term property investment.Its growing infrastructure and demand for housing continue to attract homeowners and investors alike.

  • Lifestyle Appeal: Cool climate, scenic views of Ngong Hills, quiet residential neighborhoods
  • Connectivity: Easy access to Nairobi CBD via Ngong Road and Southern Bypass
  • Property Types: Residential plots, standalone homes, gated communities, apartments
  • Investment Potential: Rising land values and strong demand from commuters working in Nairobi
  • Development: Expanding road network, new housing estates, and commercial centers
Student Hostels Residential Plots Apartments Commercial Spaces
View Ngong Properties

Syokimau – The Modern Suburban Investment Hub

Syokimau’s close proximity to Nairobi, JKIA, and the SGR has made it a preferred location for professionals and families seeking modern suburban living. The area offers strong rental demand and steady property value growth.

  • Strategic Location: Near Nairobi CBD, JKIA, and the Standard Gauge Railway
  • Connectivity: Direct access via Mombasa Road and Expressway
  • Property Types: Apartments, maisonettes, gated communities
  • Investment Potential: High rental demand from airport and city workers.
  • Development: Modern estates, schools, and shopping centers
Residential Plots Residential Estates Apartments Gated Communities
View Syokimau Properties

Ongata Rongai – The Affordable Urban Growth Hub

Ongata Rongai’s proximity to Nairobi and major learning institutions has made it a popular choice for students, young professionals, and families. The area offers relatively affordable housing with strong rental demand and rapid urban expansion.

  • Planned Development: Well-organized city with good infrastructure
  • Education & Population: Near major universities with a large student population
  • Property Types: Apartments, bedsitters, residential plots
  • Investment Potential: High rental demand from students and commuters
  • Connectivity: Easy access to Nairobi via Magadi Road

Kilimani – The Prime Urban Lifestyle & Investment District

Kilimani’s central location and vibrant lifestyle have made it one of Nairobi’s most sought-after neighborhoods for residential and mixed-use developments. The area attracts professionals and expatriates seeking convenience and modern living.

  • Development: Modern high-rises and continuous urban redevelopment
  • Lifestyle Appeal: Trendy restaurants, malls, and entertainment spots
  • Property Types: High-rise apartments, serviced residences, mixed-use developments
  • Investment Potential: Strong rental yields from professionals and expatriates
  • Connectivity:Quick access to CBD via Ngong Road and Ring Road

Athi River - The Industrial and Logistics Hub

Athi River's proximity to Nairobi and Mombasa Road, along with its industrial parks, makes it ideal for commercial and logistics investments. The area is growing rapidly with new residential developments.

  • Logistics Hub: Strategic location on Mombasa Road corridor
  • Industrial Parks: Home to major manufacturing and logistics companies
  • Property Types: Industrial land, worker housing, commercial spaces
  • Investment Potential: High demand from industrial workforce
  • Growth: Rapid development of new residential estates
