Areas We Serve

Specialized knowledge of Kenya's fastest-growing property markets

Kitengela - The Family-Friendly Investment Hub

Kitengela has transformed from a small trading center to one of Nairobi's most sought-after satellite towns. Located just 30km south of Nairobi, it offers the perfect balance of urban convenience and peaceful living.

  • Prime Location: 30 minutes from Nairobi via Mombasa Road
  • Infrastructure: Excellent road network, shopping malls, schools, hospitals
  • Property Types: Gated communities, standalone homes, apartments, plots
  • Investment Potential: High appreciation rates, strong rental demand
  • Lifestyle: Family-friendly with parks, recreational facilities

Juja - The University Town with Investment Potential

Juja's proximity to JKUAT University and Thika Superhighway has made it a hotspot for student accommodation and residential development. The area offers affordable property options with excellent growth potential.

  • Education Hub: Home to JKUAT University with high student population
  • Connectivity: Direct access to Thika Superhighway
  • Property Types: Student hostels, residential plots, apartments
  • Investment Potential: Consistent rental income from students
  • Development: Rapid infrastructure growth and new estates
Student Hostels Residential Plots Apartments Commercial Spaces
View Juja Properties

Ruiru - The Industrial and Residential Powerhouse

Ruiru's strategic location between Nairobi and Thika, coupled with its industrial base, makes it ideal for both residential and commercial investments. The area is experiencing rapid growth with new developments.

  • Industrial Base: Home to manufacturing plants and industries
  • Strategic Location: Between Nairobi and Thika on Thika Road
  • Property Types: Industrial plots, residential estates, commercial spaces
  • Investment Potential: High demand from industrial workers
  • Amenities: Shopping centers, schools, hospitals
Industrial Plots Residential Estates Commercial Spaces Gated Communities
View Ruiru Properties

Thika - The Planned City with Excellent Infrastructure

Thika's transformation into a modern city with excellent infrastructure makes it attractive for families and investors. The area offers planned communities and reliable amenities.

  • Planned Development: Well-organized city with good infrastructure
  • Amenities: Excellent schools, hospitals, shopping malls
  • Property Types: Gated communities, townhouses, commercial plots
  • Investment Potential: Steady appreciation, strong rental market
  • Lifestyle: Peaceful environment with urban conveniences
Gated Communities Townhouses Commercial Plots Residential Estates
View Thika Properties

Athi River - The Industrial and Logistics Hub

Athi River's proximity to Nairobi and Mombasa Road, along with its industrial parks, makes it ideal for commercial and logistics investments. The area is growing rapidly with new residential developments.

  • Logistics Hub: Strategic location on Mombasa Road corridor
  • Industrial Parks: Home to major manufacturing and logistics companies
  • Property Types: Industrial land, worker housing, commercial spaces
  • Investment Potential: High demand from industrial workforce
  • Growth: Rapid development of new residential estates
