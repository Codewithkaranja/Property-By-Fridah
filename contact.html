Frequently Asked Questionns

Common questions about contacting Property by Fridah

What's the best way to contact you?

WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us (+254 721 911 181). We respond within minutes during business hours. For detailed inquiries, you can also use the contact form or email.

How quickly do you respond?

WhatsApp messages: Within 15 minutes during business hours. Phone calls: Immediate if available. Emails: Within 24 hours. Contact form submissions: Within 12 hours.

Do you work with diaspora clients?

Yes, we specialize in assisting Kenyans abroad. We offer virtual property tours, handle documentation digitally, and provide regular updates. WhatsApp is ideal for diaspora clients across different time zones.

Can I schedule a site visit?

Absolutely! Site visits can be scheduled through WhatsApp, phone call, or the contact form. We typically schedule visits between 9 AM - 5 PM, but weekend and evening visits can be arranged.

What information should I provide?

When contacting us, please include: 1) Property type you're looking for, 2) Preferred location, 3) Budget range, 4) Timeline for purchase, and 5) Any specific requirements.

Is there a consultation fee?

No, initial consultations are completely free. We only earn commission when you successfully purchase a property through us. There are no hidden fees or charges for our guidance services.
